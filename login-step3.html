Complete Your Interest Profiler

The O*NET Interest Profiler has 30 questions about work activities that some people do on their jobs.
Read each question carefully and decide how would you feel about doing each type of work:

Try NOT to think about:
  • If you have enough education or training to do the work; or
  • How much money you would make doing the work.
  • Just think about if you would like or dislike doing the work
THIS IS NOT A TEST!
  • There are no right or wrong answers!
  • Please take your time answering the questions. There is no need to rush!
  • You are learnign about your interests, so that you can explore work you might like and find rewarding!
  • On the next screen, please answer each answer before continuing
  • There are 30 questions in all. you can change your answers any time.
